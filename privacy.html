Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

Form N-400 Civics Practice does not collect, sell, or share personal data. There is no account, no login, no payment, no advertising, no analytics, and no tracking. Everything you do in the app stays on your device.

What the app does NOT do

  • No account required to use the app.
  • No login or sign-in.
  • No advertisements.
  • No analytics or telemetry services.
  • No usage tracking — not by us, not by third parties.
  • No payment, in-app purchases, or subscriptions.
  • No collection of personal data (name, email, address, date of birth, photos, contacts, location, identifiers, etc.).
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data.

What stays on your device

Your study activity is processed entirely on your device:

  • Quiz answers and scores are kept in app memory while you are using the app and are discarded when you close it.
  • Optional choices such as your selected route, language, or U.S. state are kept only in memory for the current session.
  • No background sync, no cloud save, no server upload.

External links

The Resources tab inside the app, and any other USCIS links on this site, open official USCIS pages in your device's default browser. We do not embed those pages or proxy any traffic. Once you tap an external link, the destination website's own privacy practices apply — see the USCIS site for their policy.

Children

The app is intended for adults preparing for the U.S. naturalization civics test. It does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy materially changes, the Last updated date at the top will reflect the new revision. Older versions are tracked in the repository history.
